About Us

 

We are ZheyGo squad , a passionate crew obsessed with connecting people with incredible food. Our mission is to make deliciousness effortless, delivering restaurant quality straight to your doorstep.
At Zhaygo, we believe that dining is not just about food; it's about creating unforgettable experiences that bring people together. Nestled in the heart of Thimphu, our restaurant is more than just a place to eat – it's a culinary journey infused with passion, creativity, and a commitment to excellence.

Our

Story

Founded in 2024, Zhaygo began as a dream shared by Yeshi and Sonam, two food enthusiasts with a vision to redefine the dining scene in Thimphu. What started as a humble eatery has since evolved into a beloved culinary destination, known for its exceptional cuisine, warm hospitality, and inviting ambiance.
Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, catching up with friends, or simply craving a delicious meal, we invite you to join us at Zhaygo. Come savor the flavors, soak in the atmosphere, and experience hospitality at its finest. We can't wait to welcome you into our family.

ZhayGo's Growth Strategy: Partnering with Banks and Expanding

Restaurant Network in Bhutan

To enhance customer convenience and drive growth, ZhayGo will pursue strategic partnerships with Bhutanese banks. These collaborations will offer co-branded benefits to both ZhayGo users and bank customers, streamlining the online food ordering experience. Additionally, ZhayGo will expand its restaurant network to ensure broader menu options and wider geographic coverage, enabling users to order any cuisine, anytime, anywhere within Bhutan.
